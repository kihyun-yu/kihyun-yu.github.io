Hi, I am a Ph.D. student in Industrial and Systems Engineering at KAIST, advised by [Prof. Dabeen Lee](https://dabeenl.github.io/).

My research focuses on **safe reinforcement learning (RL) theory**. In particular, I design algorithms for **constrained MDPs** with provable guarantees under various settings, such as adversarial, linear, and average-reward formulations. I am also broadly interested in sequential decision-making, including bandit algorithms, online convex optimization, and their real-world applications.
